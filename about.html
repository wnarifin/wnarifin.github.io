“There are three kinds of lies: lies, damned lies, and statistics.” - Anonymous

I am Wan Nor Arifin, a lecturer at Unit of Biostatistics and Research Methodology, School of Medical Sciences, Universiti Sains Malaysia. I teach medical statistics and some other interesting stuffs. I am mainly interested in the development and validation of measurement tools, especially those for use in clinical and public health settings. I am also interested in R (statistical) programming language and promoting its use in medical statistics.

My academic profiles can be found here: USM, Research Gate
