“There are three kinds of lies: lies, damned lies, and statistics.” - Anonymous

I am Wan Nor Arifin, a lecturer at Biostatistics and Research Methodology Unit, School of Medical Sciences, Universiti Sains Malaysia. I teach medical statisticsand some other interesting subjects. I am mainly interested in the development and validation of measurement tools and machine learning, especially for use in clinical and public health settings. I am also interested in R statistical programming language and promoting its use in medical statistics. Of note, he uses R everyday, even as a desk calculator.
